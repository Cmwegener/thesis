Four bouys near our four field sites were used to collect data about the bay. Data covers 01/01/2017 to 12/31/2019. Bouys are located at (from north to south) China Camp, EOS, Richardson Bay, and Fort Point.

Additional information for each bouy: -China Camp (ppt) https://coastalobservations.sfsu.edu/chinacamp Data was incomplete last time I checked.

-EOS(ppt) https://oceanview.pfeg.noaa.gov/erddap/tabledap/rtcctdRTCysi.html 01-01-2017 to 11-14-2019 Every 6 hours Removed 2 data points from Aug 3 2019 (sal = 161 and 999) for sal

-NERR Richardson Bay Matt Ferner and Anna Deck sent me the data

-Fort Point (ppt) http://boon.ucdavis.edu/data_access.html?tab=recdata Hourly data, 01-01-2017 to 12-31-2019
