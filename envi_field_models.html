Envirnoment and field model analysis

Chelsey Wegener

16 July, 2021

Goal of this markdown: Using a AIC model ranking to analyze my evironmental and field data.

I am addressing research question #3:
Is variation in salinity, air temperature, or pH associated with changes in abundances or reproductive effort of F. distichus populations in SFE? > compare models and see which ones have the most evidence (based on fit of model and evidence)
- assumptions: environmental variables matter (lit evidence and prior knowledge)
- Environmental variables have been filtered for tides

AIC link:
https://www.statology.org/aic-in-r/

Next step:
- Do the same process but with a two month lag

Models
Ill try the same model list with a one month lag and then a two month lag. Focusing on 1) daily salinity range, daily minimum, and low salinity events, 2) daily air temperature range, daily maximum, and high air temperature events, 3) daily pH range and high pH events, and 4) adult cover (large thalli density). Previously I tested both raw and square root transformed for juvenile abundance and the untransformed data fit better and had a higher R^2.

  1. juv ~ adult cover + daily salinity range + daily air temp range + daily pH range + adult cover : daily air temp range
  2. juv ~ adult cover + low salinity event + high air temp event + high pH event + adult cover : high air temp event
  3. juv ~ adult cover + min daily salinity + max daily air temp + daily pH range + adult cover : max daily air temp
  4. juv ~ adult cover + min daily salinity + max daily air temp + high pH event + adult cover : max daily air temp
  5. juv ~ adult cover + daily salinity range + daily air temp range + adult cover : daily air temp range
  6. juv ~ adult cover + low salinity event + high air temp event + adult cover : high air temp event
  7. juv ~ adult cover + min daily salinity + max daily air temp + adult cover : max daily air temp
  8. juv ~ adult cover + daily salinity range + daily air temp range
  9. juv ~ adult cover + low salinity event + high air temp event
  10. juv ~ adult cover + min daily salinity + max daily air temp
  11. juv ~ adult cover + max daily air temp + adult cover : max daily air temp
  12. juv ~ adult cover + daily air temp range + adult cover : daily air temp range
  13. juv ~ adult cover + high air temp event + adult cover : high air temp event
  14. juv ~ adult cover + max daily air temp
  15. juv ~ adult cover + daily air temp range
  16. juv ~ adult cover + high air temp event
  17. juv ~ daily salinity range + daily pH range
  18. juv ~ low salinity event + high pH event
